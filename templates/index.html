About me

Welcome to my LinkedIn page!

My name is Raz Machlev, I'm 26 years old from Tel Aviv, Israel. I've graduated with highest distinction my B. Sc. degree in mechanical engineering and mechatronics at Ariel university. My final project was a research about optimization of micro-turbines (Piezoelectric electric energy harvesting) by controlling air vortices.

I have been working for over 4 years in the marketing industry in companies and as self employed.

Nowadays, I work as a control system and automation engineer for Automatica control technologies LTD, an Israeli manufacturer for automated industrial assembly lines and machines, located in Kfar Saba. My role is designing the control systems for industrial assembly lines (PLC programming, motion systems, robotics, Vision and HMI), from the planning stage to the run up stage at customers' factories in Israel and around the world.

In addition to my knowledge and experience with control systems and programming, I have a very deep understanding of many areas of engineering, in particular energy, aeronautics, transportation and automobile industries.

I love music (play piano and guitar), art, travel and always happy to meet new people and friends. If you find my profile interesting you'r more than welcome to hit me up and discuss more!
